Frequently Asked Questions

What are the metro operating hours?

The metro operates daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, including holidays.

How do I report a lost item?

Contact our helpline or visit the lost and found office at any metro station with details of your lost item.

How can I get a metro smart card?

Visit any metro station's ticket counter with your national ID card and a passport-size photograph.
